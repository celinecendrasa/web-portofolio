King Mastro

King Mastro is a stationery shop that provides 40+ brands of office and school stationery. King Mastro has been established since 2019 and is located in Alam Sutera, Tangerang.

I want to introduce the concept of a “helper” for King Mastro. Here, “King” is redefined to shift the perception of an arrogant king into a figure that helps us (customers) to complete our journey and provides hope in reaching our goals.

HEX: #ECECD8

RGB: 236, 236, 216

CMYK: 7, 3, 16, 0

Warm & positive

HEX: #0D512B

RGB: 13, 81, 43

CMYK: 89, 40, 96, 42

Reliability, calm, & balance

Meaning

symbolizes a King who helps us open the path toward our goals.

Door Open

represents the wings provided by the King to reach those goals. The wings here also represent King Mastro’s products, which help us fulfill our objectives.

Wing

represents the aspirations behind the door. Hope signifies that every customer who purchases supplies from King Mastro gains hope to pursue their personal goals, while Reflection represents the customer themselves.

The logo incorporates organic shapes to illustrate a King who is flexible and not rigid in offering help.

Movie Poster

This poster is a recreation of Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone with Indonesian culture’s touch. "Hermione's" costume is replaced with batik, adding a local nuance to the iconic character. Wayang elements are also included, enriching the design with Javanese culture. Printed on A2-sized albatross paper, this poster combines the magical essence of Harry Potter with a touch of Indonesian tradition.

In addition to the batik costume, the poster incorporates elements of wayang (shadow puppetry), which are an integral part of Javanese culture. The use of these elements enriches the overall design, offering a deeper connection to Indonesia's artistic traditions. Printed on A2-sized albatross paper, this poster merges the enchanting world of Harry Potter with the timeless beauty of Indonesian heritage, creating a captivating fusion that honors both the magical and cultural realms.
